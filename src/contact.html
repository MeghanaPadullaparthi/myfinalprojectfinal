Why is security needed?

Authored By: Meghana Padullaparthi

At a basic level your privacy is constantly being invaded by the sites you visit, your ISP, your government and even the services you use like Google or Facebook. As of right now, the biggest consequences have been selling all of your data to third-party marketers. Some people also fear certain things like leaks of personal information, which is a valid concern.
